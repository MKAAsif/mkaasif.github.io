Md. Kawsar Ahmed Asif

Lecturer in Mathematics, Department of General Education, Canadian University of Bangladesh.

Md. Kawsar Ahmed Asif completed his M.Sc. (Thesis) and B.S. (Hon’s) in Mathematics from the University of Dhaka. He has been working as a Lecturer in Mathematics under the General Education Department at the Canadian University of Bangladesh since June 21, 2022. Previously, he worked as a Lecturer in Mathematics under the Basic Science Division at the World University of Bangladesh from 6th May 2019 to 30th June 2022.

As a career objective, he wants to build up an academic or research career in Mathematics to utilize his capabilities to the fullest benefit of his country. He is a lifetime member of the Bangladesh Mathematical Society (BMS).

Research Interest: Data Science, Operations Research, Operations Management, Numerical Analysis, Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, Game Theory, and Discrete Mathematics.

Programming Skills: MATLAB, Python, C, Fortran, and Mathematica.

Extra-Curricular Activities: Calligraphy, Photography, Painting, and Crafting.
